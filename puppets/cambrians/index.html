Cambrian Overview

Components 419
Durability 22/10
Strength Physical: 11/10
Psionic: 18/10
Magical: 16/10
Specials Ally-protecting liqeous medium
Spawns lesser titans
Personality Benevolent and gentle

Cambrians are enormous titans that suffuse their environment in antibody-rich effluvia, using it to protect their allies or to sculpt into lesser titans to further their agendas of support and disaster relief. They are the hosts of Cornucopiae, Auroras, and Seraphs.

Physical Description

Cambrians are truly colossal puppets, standing taller than even Perditioners at a breathtaking 70' (2134cm) before considering the trees on their backs. They are also considerably bulky, standing on six legs to more comfortably distribute their weight. A Cambrian's most defining feature its the massive tree on its back, which grows throughout its body and acts as a locus for the most powerful of its world-recovering magics. Cambrians also have several large gouges in their body which constantly leak some amount of effluvia out into their envrionment, pouring over the ground as a way for a Cambrian to spread its influence.

Behaviour

Cambrians are ultra-heavy support puppets with an emphasis on protecting and healing their allies, though they rarely see direct use in combat. When it comes down to it, however, their primary tool is their effluvia: a protein-rich slurry of biological chemicals intended for keeping living creatures alive and protected. While it appears as a fluid, it can be breathed by all manner of respiratory systems (lungs, skin, gills, et cetera), and protects from extreme heat and cold. The effluvia can bind to itself in a vaccuum, allowing normally atmospheric creatures to survive within. It can also decompose into soil according to the Cambrian's will, allowing it to restore desolate land to a replenished state. Cambrians employ this effluvia constantly, even producing and leaking it when sleeping. The more area the effluvia covers, the bigger the Cambrian's effective range of influence.

In terms of tools beyond their physical effluvia, Cambrians have a healthy variety of verdant magic available to them; in particular, conjuring plants. This allows them to reclaim wasteland even if no biological samples survive. While they cannot earthshape like Perditioners can, Cambrians can erode stones into soils by conjuring lichen to restore environments over time. Cambrians are capable of producing broad auras that prevent the development of diseases and encourage the recovery of organisms, allowing them to accelerate the natural healing process of a damaged ecosystem even once the bulk of the devastation is clear.

Lastly, Cambrians can sculpt lesser titans out of their effluvia to aid them with reclamation and caring for those impacted by the devastation they're designed to clean up. Cornucopiae provide food, Auroras provide light and warmth, Seraphs aid in the further healing and recovery of that which is affected.

Genetic Description

Being Titans, Cambrians share many epigenetic triggers with Perditioners. They also share a healthy amount of triggers with Reconcilants and many other puppets that employ subpuppets. Effluvia also bears a resemblance to unspecialized puppet slurry, suggesting a link between Cambrians and the nature of puppets as a whole. Despite this, Cambrians are never seen sculpting their effluvia into other puppets or subpuppets; only their own lesser titans.

Metanarrative

Like Perditioners before them, Cambrians are meant to invoke a certain "biblical presence" due to their titanic status and powerful abilities. The two types represent a yin and yang: Perditioners destroy, Cambrians recover and create. Cambrians have definitely taken more influence from biblical events (particularly Christianity, what with the flooding and angels and whatnot), though any kind of resemblance is probably fairly superficial as I myself am not religious.
